Why I Made One:

2020 Felt Like a Nightmare

When the pandemic hit, honestly, it was kinda rough. As it was for most people, my way of couping with it, was to make a YouTube Channel. It gave me access to continue talking to people about the things I love and enjoy. I love my hobbies and I love always being involved with something.

Opening up the YouTube Channel didn't just open the door to getting close with my subscribers. It also opened up ways to learn new things. In the past couple months I've learned how to edit, design, market, talk in front of people, build self-confidence, learn to write better, time manage better, and learn to appreciate my work space more.

Starting a YouTube channel, just really helped me build myself during a time where it felt like things were empty. I am very grateful to have and enjoy what I do. And I hope you do too if you happen to stop by my channel. I love making people smile and I believe my YouTube channel really shows my personality.
